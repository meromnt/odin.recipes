Sashimi recipe

Description

Sashimi is a japanese delicacy,the word sashimi means pierced body

Ingredients

  • about 150g pre person sashimi-grade tuna or salmon
  • wasabi
  • japanese soy sauce
  • picked ginger
  • 1 mooli
  • for decoration shiso leaves or micro leaves

Steps

  1. Choosing your sashimi ingredients
  2. Cutting the fish into sashimi slices
  3. Plating the sashimi
  4. Serve
