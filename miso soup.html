Miso soup recipe

Description

What is Miso Soup? Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup made primarily of miso paste, dashi (broth), and additional ingredients such as vegetables, seaweed, and tofu.

Ingredients

  • water
  • piece of kombu
  • tamari
  • white miso paste
  • silken tofu
  • cup chopped scallions
  • dried seaweed
