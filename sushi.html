Sushi Recipe

Description

sushi at it's core , classical japanese food

Ingredients

  • raw fish
  • nori sheet
  • sushi rice
  • seaweed
  • avocado sliced
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. prep the rice and nori
  2. spread the rice on the nori
  3. add fillings to the bottom quarter of the nori
  4. roll the sushi part way
  5. roll the sushi the rest of the way
  6. move to cutting board
  7. cut the sushi
  8. serve
